Background History

The name 'Krish Dance', originated from the Indian hindu God named Krishna and his passion for dancing. He believed that dancing is a great way to express a story, your emotions and an art ike no other.

Best Dancers Around!

We have the best dancers from all around New Zealand and some International Dancers as well. Our studio is located in the wonderful region of Wellington and right at the heart of Wellington CBD.

Positive Environment

Our studio believes in a friendly and positive environment. It does not support any form of bullying or tolerate misbahviour of any type. Our aim is to give our dancers the best experience and teaching they will ever receive.
